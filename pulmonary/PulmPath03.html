Pulmonary Microscopic Pathology Image 3
This is a pentachrome stained slide (originally 20x) with a very thickened basement membrane, smooth muscle hypertrophy, and goblet-cell hyperplasia.  There is a large submucous gland and mucus lining the epithelium.  There is minimal airway inflammation (no eosinophils, no neutrophils, and only scanty patches of submucosal lymphocytes).
