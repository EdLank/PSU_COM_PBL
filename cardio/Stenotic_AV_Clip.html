Stenotic Aortic Valve Echo Clip
An echo clip of a heart with a stenotic aortic valve.  Note the large echogenic masses at the aortic vale plane, with very limited movement.  These are from heavily calcified aortic valve leaflets.  There is only a small opening between the cusps in systole.  The movie will play once it has loaded, but may not work on all versions of all browsers and all settings.
