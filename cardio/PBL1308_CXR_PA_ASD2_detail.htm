ASD 2 CXR

Enlarged pulmonary artery (blue arrow.  The right and left main pulmonary arteries (yellow arrows).  The high pulmonary artery pressure creates a high RV afterload, so the right atrium is also enlarged (magenta ellipse).  The left atrium is also enlarged and causes a right sided "double density" red ellipse).  The left heart border, usually concave, loses the concavity at the left atrium, demonstrating the large left atrium( red arrow).  The large left atrium also raises the left mainstem bronchus, splaying the carina yellow angle.  The large RV displaces the LV apex leftward (pink arrow).
