Thoracoscopy

Thoroscopic images of the left thorax in a patient with a spontaneous left pneumothorax.  The camera has entered at the left anterior axillary line, viewing toward the apex.  The sternum is at the left.  A stapler is inserted through a separate thorocotomy.  It applies 6 parallel rows of staples, and then cuts between the 3rd and 4throws, removing the section with blebs.  In the last image, a section of lung is partially resected, leaving 3 rows of staples on each side.
