Dental Film

This film demonstrates dental decay. Tooth #2 (left most tooth, the upper second molar) has a temporary medicated filling.  It shows a periapical abscess star in the space between the blue arcs.  Tooth #3 (first molar, and the probable cause of this bacteremia), also has a restorative filling, with extensive decay of the crown, (red circle) that has encroached on the pulp.  The necrosis spreads deep and causes the periapical abscess, between the red arcs.  Tooth #4 shows a restorative filling with decay beneath it (yellow circle).  There is also evidence of a healed periapical abscess between the yellow arcs.  Tooth #5 has had a prior root canal magenta arrow.
