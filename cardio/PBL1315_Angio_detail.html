The left image has very good contrast because the malformation is in the lung parenchyma, so it is against lung density.  The right image is a digital subtraction of the image without contrast (not shown) from the image with contrast (on left), almost eliminating immobile unchanging structures.  Notice the disappearance of other chest structures that have not moved or changed between images, but a very faint ghost of the ribs.  The catheter has moved between images, so it leaves a dark versus a light image of the moving catheter.
